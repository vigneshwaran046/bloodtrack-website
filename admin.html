HOW TO BECOME A VOLUNTEER:

  • First join in our organisation by registering with correct Gmail id.
  • In the login form you should select the register option.
  • Check whether the given gmail id and password are correct and don't forget it.
  • After submitting you will become a volunteer.

BENEFITS OF VOLUNTEER:

  • You can able to see the data stored in our database.
  • After seeing the requests in our data base you can share this to your friends to raise request for the blood donation form, then the person will be contacted.
  • You can join with our team members and you can also do social services.
